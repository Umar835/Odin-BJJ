Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

What is BJJ?

BJJ stands for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. BJJ is a grappling-based martial art which focuses on the skill of controlling a resisting opponent in ways that force him to submit.

  • BJJ was found by Esai Maeda in the early 1900s as a weaponless method of defense for Samurai warriors.
  • It takes about 8-13 years to become a black belt in BJJ.
  • Brazilian jiu-jitsu can be done with or without a gi.

Benefits of BJJ

  1. You can build incredible self-defence skills.
  2. Improves confidence.
  3. You become more disciplined.
  4. Improves problem solving skills.
